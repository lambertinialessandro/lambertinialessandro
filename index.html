What is the "Percorso d'Eccellenza"?

The "Percorso d'Eccellenza" (Path of Excellence) aims to enhance
the training of students enrolled, deserving and interested in in-depth activities.
The path of excellence consists of training activities in addition to those of the study course in which the student is enrolled.

Percorso d'Eccellenza (200 hours):

  • Theoretical and methodological activities: (100 hours):

    • Attendance of PhD courses (not taught in the Master's Degree)

    • Attendance of an advanced summer school

  • Projects (100 hours):

    • Development and research activities in the industrial field and attendance
      of specialized courses with internships related to R&D projects
